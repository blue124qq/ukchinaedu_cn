Is Downloading YouTube Videos Legal?

Almost five billion videos are watched around the world each day, so it’s not surprising that people would want to have an offline version of their favorite videos.

However, YouTube doesn’t want anyone downloading the videos on its site for these reasons:

  • YouTube makes money from paid ads on its site and also from YouTube Premium subscription (assuming it is available in your country).
  • YouTube creators also make money from the site and so, YouTube is stern on protecting the content of its creators.

Following these reasons, it’s only fair to consider the legality of downloading YouTube videos.

What Does the Law Say?

The United States copyright law emphasizes that it is illegal to make a copy of content if you do not have the permission of the copyright owner. That applies to every form of content, both public or private use.

However, in reality, the chance of facing a criminal lawsuit, especially as a private user, is very slim. But that doesn’t change the fact that the law doesn’t support downloading YouTube videos.

What Does YouTube Say?

YouTube doesn’t regard downloading the videos on its platform as illegal per se. It is just against their terms of service. That’s why you won’t see any application on Google play store whose sole function is downloading YouTube videos.

YouTube has never lashed at a user for downloading videos off its platform. Even though they once threatened to take youtube-mp3.org, a popular converter site, to court in an attempt to discourage people from ripping music off its platform free of charge. However, nothing ever came of it.

However, the struggle with videos that can be downloaded on YouTube is that these videos can only be accessed on the YouTube platform, either through the website or the mobile application, and cannot be converted to mp3 and saved or shared outside the YouTube platform.

In a nutshell, downloading YouTube videos is not illegal. However, it does open you up to potential copyright infringement.

How Do You Download YouTube Videos with Browser Add-ons?

Unlike video downloading sites where you paste the URL of your favorite YouTube video to download it, browser add-ons offer a more efficient, convenient, and effective alternative.

All you need to do is to download and install the program on your computer and the add-on downloads the video of your choice in a straightforward process. However, keep in mind that browser add-ons work best only on a computer.

Let’s see how to install browser add-ons in these steps below.

Installing a Browser Add-on for YouTube Videos on Chrome

As you already know, YouTube is solely invested in keeping users on the YouTube platform for as long as they can and since Google owns YouTube and Chrome is one of Google’s numerous products, installing add-ons on chrome browsers may not be as easy and straightforward as other browsers.

Let’s see the steps involved:

  • Go to the site of your preferred browser add-on and click on the download button. The extension would be downloaded to your device.
  • Open a new tab on your chrome browser and click on “Settings“. Go to “Tools” and click on “Extensions“.
  • At the top right side of the screen, you would see an option to “Enable Developer Mode“. Click on it.
  • After you enable developer mode, a menu will be displayed that allows you to load the extension. Click on the “Load Button.”
  • Locate the downloaded extension in your computer files and click on it. That should install the add-on to your chrome browser.

Installing a Browser Add-on for YouTube Videos on Other Browsers

Browsers like Opera, Safari, Firefox are not as complex as Chrome. They are much easier and straightforward.

Let’s see the steps involved:

  • Go to the site of your preferred YouTube video browser add-ons and click the download button.
  • Once downloaded, locate the file on your computer and double-click on it to install it. You could also right-click on the file and select “Run as Administrator“.
  • After a successful installation, the browser add-on will be added directly to your browser.

Downloading YouTube Videos with a Browser Add-on

After successfully installing your browser add-on on your computer, you can follow these steps to download YouTube videos to your computer.

  • Open the YouTube website and choose a video you want to download.

How Do I Download YouTube Videos with a Virtual Desktop?

Using a Virtual Desktop to download your YouTube videos is very easy and straightforward.

However, it is not limited to only Youtube videos as it can download videos from other social platforms such as Facebook, Megavideo, DailyMotion, Vimeo, and Metacafe.

You don’t need any YouTube video downloader app or plugin and you can download videos in different formats and quality straight from your browser.

I’ve outlined two methods to help you download your favorite YouTube videos with a Virtual desktop. Let’s look at them below.

Method 1

  • Go to the YouTube website and select the video you want to download.
  • Click on the video and allow it to load fully. Once loaded, allow it to play for a few seconds and then pause the video.
  • Go to the address bar, which is at the top of the site. Click on the URL of the video and type in “vd” after “www.” but before “youtube.com” After typing, click the Enter button on your computer keyboard.
  • Once you click Enter, you will be redirected to the VDYoutube website.
  • On the VDYoutube website, scroll down to see the different quality and resolutions available for download.
  • Choose your most preferred video quality and resolution. Please note that the higher the number of resolutions, the brighter the quality of the video. For example, a 1280 x 720 video resolution has a brighter video quality than a 640 x 360 video resolution.
  • After deciding on your preferred video resolution, click on the “Download button” beside it.
  • You will be redirected to another tab where your video will start playing by itself. Pause the video and right-click on the screen. Then, select “Save Video As” in the pop up that appears.
  • The video will immediately start downloading on your desktop. After successfully downloading, a pop up will appear on the screen with a default name, “videoplayback“. Change the default name to your preferred file name. Type in the name and select your preferred location. Then, click “Save.”
  • You can also click on the three dots on the video and click on “Download” in the menu that appears. However, this feature downloads and saves the video directly on your computer. You will have to search for the file in your Downloads folder to rename it.

Method 2

  • Log onto the YouTube website and select the video you wish to download.
  • Allow the video to load and play for a few seconds. Then pause the video.
  • Go to the address bar on the YouTube website and copy the URL on the video.
  • In a new tab, open the VDYouTube website. In the search bar, paste the URL and click Enter on your computer keyboard or the “GO Button” beside the search bar.
  • The video will show up in different quality and resolutions. Click on ” Download” beside your preferred format and your video will start downloading instantly.

How Do I Download YouTube Videos with a VLC Media Player?

The multimedia player, with the icon of an orange traffic cone, VLC media player, is one of the best multimedia players and has become everybody’s go-to because of the following reasons:

  • It is free.
  • It is supported on numerous platforms such as Windows, Mac, and Linux.
  • It supports various formats of video and audio files.
  • It doesn’t require any additional add-on to function.
  • It doesn’t have ads, and it’s free from viruses.

However, an added advantage that few people seem to miss is the ability of VLC media players to download YouTube videos.

Over the years, VideoLAN organization, developers of VLC media player has recorded a total of over 3 billion downloads on the platform.

What does this tell you?

Not only is downloading from a VLC media player easy and straightforward, it conveniently converts the YouTube video to a high-resolution mp4 version which can easily be saved on your computer.

Here’s what you will need when using a VLC media player to download YouTube videos.

  • An active internet connection
  • YouTube video link
  • A VLC media player
  • A web browser

Let’s look at the very easy steps that can help you download YouTube videos with a VLC media player. But take note that these steps work only on a computer device.

Steps to Download YouTube Videos with a VLC Media Player

  1. Get the VLC media player if you haven’t already. You can download it here. Click on the downward arrow beside the “Download VLC” to select your preferred device.
  1. After a successful download, open the VLC media player.
  1. In the Navigation bar of your VLC media player, click on “Media” and then select “Open Network Stream” for Windows users. For Mac users, click on “File” and then select “Open media.”
  1. Go over to YouTube and highlight the URL of the YouTube video that you will like to download. The URL is located in the address bar, at the top of your browser. Then right-click and select “Copy” from the dropdown, or you could simply type Ctrl + C on your keyboard if using a Windows computer or Command + C if using a Mac computer.
  1. Return to the VLC media player and click on “Network” along the top menu bar.
  1. A URL field will appear on the screen. Paste the copied URL from YouTube videos onto that space. You can do this by right-clicking on the empty URL field and selecting Paste from the dropdown, or you can simply type Ctrl + V if using a Windows computer or Command + V if using a Mac.
  1. After pasting the YouTube video URL into the URL field, click “Play” for Windows users or “Open” for Mac users. That will cause the YouTube video to begin playing in your VLC media player.
  1. Select the YouTube video information in the navigation bar. 
  • For Windows Users: In the navigation bar of your VLC media player, click on “Tools” and then select “Codec Information” on the dropdown.
  • For Mac Users: In the navigation bar of your VLC media player, click on “Windows” and then select “Media Information” on the dropdown.
  1. After selecting the YouTube video information, a pop-up will appear on your screen. Highlight and copy the address on the location bar. The Location bar can be found at the bottom of the pop-up image in the General bar. It’s a very long address so here’s how to easily copy it. 
  • For Windows users: Right-click on the location bar and click “Select All” on the dropdown menu. The address will be highlighted by now, so right-click again and select “Copy” on the dropdown menu. 
  • For Mac users: Right-click on the location bar and select “Open URL.”
  1. Go over to your internet browser and open a new tab. Paste the text copied from the location bar on the VLC media player into the address bar on your internet browser. That will open an mp4 version of your YouTube video that can be saved on your desktop. However, Mac users should skip this step since you have selected “Open URL” in the previous step.
  1. Move your cursor to a space on the enlarged YouTube video playing on your screen. Right-click and select “Save Video As” on the dropdown menu.
  1. Change the name of the file to your preferred choice and also select your preferred video file destination. Click on “Save.”
  1. The video will be downloaded and saved on your computer at the highest resolution available. After downloading successfully, you can then open the location of the video and watch your YouTube video offline.

Best Cloud Storages for Downloaded YouTube Videos

After downloading your YouTube videos, you may decide to edit the videos to improve their visibility and create a more personal view of the video.

That’s a bit further into your creativity and so finding a good storage option for the videos is your best choice.

Downloaded and edited videos save automatically to your device storage. However, there’s a lot of risks attached to that form of storage.

And unless you don’t want to keep on downloading your videos, you must consider other options aside from the traditional phone or computer storage.

A great option will be to use cloud storage.

Over 3 billion people employ cloud services for a number of their activities including storage. That’s because cloud storage takes the hassles out of storing your video collection, especially with the ever-growing video resolutions and file sizes.

To help you decide on the best cloud storage, I’ve put together a list of our favorite cloud storage. However, before you select your cloud storage, here’s what you must look at for.

  • Security Measures: When using cloud storage, you are giving out your valuables to a third party in expectation that they will keep it safe. So, ensure that they have the necessary security measures in place to secure every byte of your videos.
  • Customer Support: No matter how amazing cloud storage is, it will still have little hitches from time to time. When these issues arise, knowing that you have reliable assistance can be very helpful. Also, inquire what hours of the day and day of the week the support is available.
  • Pricing: Cloud storage has been said to be less expensive than traditional storage systems. However, you will still need to make a deliberate choice that fits your budget when choosing cloud storage. Some cloud storage offers free storage space upon registration but would later ask for a fee either after some days or if you require more space. Others offer monthly and yearly payments to store your videos. Figure out the plan that works best for you and follow it.

Now, let’s see the best cloud storage to store your YouTube videos in detail.

1. pCloud

Most Secure Cloud Storage for Storing Videos.

pCloud, launched in 2013, has outdone itself and is one of our best choices for storing your downloaded YouTube videos.

It has an incredible interface that enables you to share and play your video files directly from pCloud on all operating systems such as Android, macOS, Windows, Linux, and iOS and a desktop app that allows automatic syncing of files across all devices into your pCloud account.

pCloud offers two-factor authentication for added security and social media backups to help you easily connect your pCloud account with any of your social media accounts and backup your media directly.

Registering an account with pCloud offers you as much as 10GB of space for free. However, pricing begins at $3.99 monthly or $47.88 yearly and offers 500 GB of space while the Premium Plus plan costs $7.99 monthly or $95.88 yearly and offers 2TB of space. pCloud also offers a one-time payment which costs $175 and $350 for Premium and Premium Plus respectively, which is valid for 99 years.

Pros

  • 10GB of space free upon registration.
  • No restrictions on file sharing.
  • Block-level file syncing, allowing for faster transfer of files.

Cons

  • No add-on services.

Register with pCloud and get a free 10GB of space for storing your favorite YouTube videos.

2. Dropbox

Most Popular Cloud Storage with Advanced Selective Sync Options.

Dropbox is a household name because as early as 2008, it was replacing the tiring USB drive storage with its cloud storage and file syncing and backup services spanning across different devices and operating systems.

Dropbox excels well in the sharing and collaboration of files in its cloud. So, you can give others access to your videos files on your cloud storage and withdraw the access at any time.

Being in the cloud storage market for so long has allowed Dropbox to evolve through different time stamps, growing in both experience and quality of service. So, you are assured that your video files are secured when you install Dropbox on your Windows, iOS, Android, and macOS device.

Dropbox offers a 2GB worth of space free for its new users. However, selecting a price helps you to access their features without any restrictions.

Pricing starts at $11.99 or $19.99 monthly for individuals, and this amount is for 2TB and 3TB of space, respectively. Business plans begin at $15 monthly for 5TB of space.

Pros

  • Advanced, elegant, and easy-to-use user interface.
  • Multiple access to files is allowed.
  • Packed with unlimited sync options.

Cons

  • It’s not the cheapest option.
  • Previous experience with a breach of security.
  • Limited free storage.

Get Dropbox here to enjoy the benefits of unlimited video sync options.

3. Sync.com

Most Flexible YouTube Video Cloud Storage with Advanced Security.

At the top of Sync.com features are its security details. It offers private, end-to-end encryption for your downloaded youtube videos.

What does it mean?

Sync.com performs encryption on your local device before the videos are transferred to the cloud. Private means that no one except you can have access to the videos in this cloud storage. End-to-end means that because you use a password which only you know for encryption, the videos cannot be decrypted after transfer.

Sync.com also provides numerous syncing options for your video files and offers an effective sharing link that can be accessed by others.

Upon registration, you get a free 5GB storage space with unlimited file transfer. However, opting for the Sync.com payment plan gives you a more expansive storage space option. At $5 monthly, you get 200GB worth of space and the top-tier plan is at $15 monthly which offers you 10000GB worth of space.

Sync.com also provides options for you to reset your password when the need arises. You can easily reset your password from the desktop app, or enable email-based password recovery.

Pros

  • High-level security.
  • Quick and easy account recovery.
  • Unlimited access to files.

Cons

  • Restricted collaboration access because of high-level security.
  • Syncing can be slow due to its high-security detail.

Open a Sync.com account here and secure your YouTube videos.

4. MediaFire

Most Preferred Cloud Storage for Sharing Large YouTube Videos.

MediaFire has been around since 2006, starting out as a file hosting website and has evolved into a cloud storage service. It offers 10GB storage space free upon registration with a file transfer limit of 20GB and allows you to share your file link to others.

MediaFire has a strict anti-piracy policy and automatically removes any video file that breaks the copyright laws.

However, security on MediaFire is relatively poor as it doesn’t offer any encryption option whatsoever. It also doesn’t have a desktop app, so it is impossible to sync video files to your computer.

MediaFire is an excellent option for storing and sharing your files and videos, but that’s just as much as it can provide – which is too low for a cloud storage service.

Pros 

  • 10GB free storage space.
  • Large file transfer limit.
  • File sharing enabled.

Cons

  • Too many ads.
  • Limited features.
  • File sync limitations.
  • Low-level security.

Get on board with MediaFire and enjoy unrestricted access to share your large YouTube video files.

5. Box

All-round Functional Cloud Storage Suitable For Businesses.

Box functionality expands beyond offering automatic file syncing and storage opportunities. They also offer secured protection for your files by providing a two-factor authentication option, offer access to integrate with multiple apps, and provide a free 10GB of space upon registration.

Although Box is suited primarily for businesses, it is also an excellent personal option for storing your youtube videos.

The web interface is very enticing and very easy to navigate. It also has a drag and drop feature that makes uploading files very easy and fast and a preview feature that allows you to check the content of the video file before transferring it.

Pricing begins at $10 monthly with a total of 100GB of space and a 5GB transfer limit. Also, note that Box imposes a 250MB file transfer limit for the free plan.

Pros

  • Mobile friendly.
  • An organized and straightforward web interface.
  • Automatic file syncing.

Cons

  • Restrictions on free account.
  • Overly expensive.

Get started with Box and enjoy a free 10GB of space to store your favorite YouTube videos.

6. MEGA

Beginner-friendly Cloud Storage for Downloaded YouTube Videos with Guaranteed Security.

Just like the name suggests, MEGA is the most generous of all the cloud storage offering 15GB of space free for registered users. It has a straightforward and very easy to use web interface supported on Windows, iOS, Linux, macOS, and Android operating systems.

MEGA provides a range of security, including end-to-end encryption that gives you exclusive access to your files on your cloud storage.

However, I’ll advise you to enable two-factor authentication and write the code in a safe place so that in cases where you forget your password, you can use the code to recover your cloud storage account.

Also, note that MEGA restricts the number of files you share monthly. It records how much data you transfer through your cloud monthly, and once you have reached your file transfer limit, your activities on the platform will be restricted. You will either have to wait for the monthly file transfer limit to reset or upgrade your plan.

Pricing begins at €4.99 monthly for 400GB with a file transfer limit of 1TB. The highest plan is worth €29.99 monthly for 16TB storage with a file transfer limit of 2TB.

Pros

  • Large free storage.
  • Most simplified and easy to use web interface.
  • Robust pricing system.
  • High-level security.

Cons

  • Limit on the size of files you can transfer.
  • Limited add-ons.

Get started with MEGA and enjoy 15GB of space free for your YouTube videos.

7. SugarSync

Most Preferred Cloud Storage for its Simplicity and Ease of Use.

SugarSync offers just about anything that you need in cloud storage. 

It allows you to sync changes made on your video files instantly and back them up on different computers and mobile devices. It also provides secured file sharing and allows you to remotely remove all synchronized files from any device.

SugarSync doesn’t offer end-to-end encryption. Instead, they use a high strength AES-256 bit encryption to secure your files and videos to and from its servers – which isn’t entirely bad.

Individual pricing begins at $7.49 monthly for 100GB of space, $9.99 monthly for 250GB of space, and $18.95 monthly for 500GB of space. Pricing for business begins at $55 monthly for 1TB of space. However, there’s a 30-day free trial on all these plans to give you an idea of what they are about.

Pros

  • Supports syncing across multiple devices.
  • Unlimited sharing options.

Cons

  • Merger web interface without much functions.
  • File sharing is occasionally slow.
  • Overly expensive.
  • Doesn’t offer two-factor authentication for logging in.

Select your preferred SugarSync plan and enjoy a 30-day free trial.
